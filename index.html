Admissions 2024

Simple steps to begin your journey at Tamil Tech University.

Application Process

01

Online Registration

Register on our portal with your basic personal and academic details.

02

Document Upload

Upload all required academic transcripts and identity documents.

03

Entrance Test (TTAT)

Appear for the Tamil Tech Admission Test conducted online or offline.

04

Final Selection

Selected candidates will be interviewed and offered admission letters.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 60% marks in Higher Secondary (10+2) education.
  • Valid score in a national or state level entrance examination.
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills required.
  • International students must submit valid TOEFL or IELTS scores.
  • Age must be between 17 and 25 years for undergraduate programs.
